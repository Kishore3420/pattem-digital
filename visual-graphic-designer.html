Visual Graphic Designer

Are you a talented and passionate Visual Graphic Designer looking to make a real difference? We’re looking for someone just like you to join our team and help design powerful, visually appealing content for both digital and print platforms.

In this role, you’ll get the chance to bring ideas to life and create unique visual experiences. Whether it’s working on branding, designing user interfaces (UI/UX), or producing motion graphics and video content, you’ll have the opportunity to work across a wide range of exciting projects. You’ll also have the opportunity to explore and incorporate the latest technologies and tools to enhance your designs and push creative boundaries.

As a Visual Graphic Designer, you’ll collaborate with cross-functional teams, contributing your design expertise to create high-quality visual content that resonates with our target audience. Whether you’re working on static or dynamic visuals, your role will be critical in defining and maintaining the visual identity of our brand.

If you’re someone who thrives on creativity, loves collaborating with others, and is excited to contribute to a team that’s always pushing the envelope, we’d love to hear from you. Help shape the future of our projects and make an impact with your design expertise.

Graphic Designer Responsibilities:

  • Apply your expertise in design principles, color theory, usability, and functionality to create visually appealing and effective designs that serve both aesthetic and practical purposes.
  • Develop and maintain a consistent brand identity across all design materials, ensuring alignment with the company’s visual standards while resonating with the target audience.
  • Create custom drawings, icons, and graphics to enhance visual storytelling and improve the overall design quality.
  • Craft compelling narratives through skilled video editing, including cuts, sound, and music. Your role involves visually communicating a story in a creative and engaging way.
  • Produce high-quality motion graphics for digital platforms like social media, websites, and videos, showcasing your expertise in animation and visual effects.
  • Use Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, etc.) to design, edit, and produce visual content. Figma proficiency is required for UI/UX design.
  • Bring fresh, innovative ideas to the table for each project, ensuring that designs are engaging and aligned with the brand’s identity and messaging.
  • Work closely with cross-functional teams such as developers, marketing, and product teams, to create design solutions that meet business goals and user needs.
  • Design intuitive, user-friendly interfaces using Figma, focusing on seamless user experiences and visually appealing designs.
  • Experience in prototyping and creating layouts or components is key.
  • Maintain pixel-perfect designs that are consistent across various platforms, ensuring high-quality and cohesive visuals.

Graphic Designer Skills:

  • 2-3 years of professional experience as a Visual Graphic Designer, Graphic Designer, or similar role.
  • Advanced proficiency in Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects, Premiere Pro, etc.) and Figma for UI/UX design.
  • Knowledge of Blender, Maya, or Cinema 4D is a plus.
  • Expertise in motion graphics, video editing, and sound/music editing, with a focus on enhancing storytelling through visuals.
  • Strong drawing abilities to create custom graphics, icons, and visuals.
  • Ability to generate innovative and engaging design solutions that capture attention and align with the brand’s identity.
  • A keen eye for design, ensuring consistency and quality across all assets.
  • Strong communication skills to present and explain design concepts clearly to stakeholders and team members.
  • A portfolio showcasing your best work in Digital & Graphic Design, including branding, UI/UX, illustration, and motion graphics.
  • Motion graphic experience is a plus.
  • Strong teamwork skills and the ability to integrate feedback into the design process effectively.

Perks and Benefits:

  • Flexible working hours.
  • Availability for Work From Home.
  • Casual clothing policies.
  • Access to fitness classes and wellness programs.
  • 5 working days a week.

Location: Bangalore [Office/Remote]

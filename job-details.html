Front-end Architect / UI Architect Job

Are you interested in a UI Architect or Front-End Architect position in Bangalore?

Exceptional front-end development is a multifaceted endeavor, and scaling it to accommodate multiple teams working on a large and complex product simultaneously presents significant challenges. As a UI Architect, your role will involve shaping the direction of modern micro-frontend architecture for Pattem Digital and Partner products. You will be instrumental in building the UI framework that empowers autonomous teams to develop independently deployable applications. Collaborating with UI leads from different divisions will be a key aspect of your responsibilities.

For detailed information about your role, please refer to our  Service Page

Familiarize Yourself with Responsibilities:

  • 10-16 years of experience in component-based architecture, specializing in technologies like React, Redux, Vue, or similar frameworks.
  • Over 6 years of hands-on development experience with enterprise-level JavaScript web applications.
  • Possess a minimum of 6 months of front-end development experience with AEM (Adobe Experience Manager).
  • Proficient in UI design, architectural concepts, and design patterns.
  • Demonstrated expertise in designing large-scale JavaScript application architectures for at least 3 years.
  • Skilled in JavaScript, architectural diagrams, design patterns, and integrating APIs.
  • Have 4-5 years of experience in customer-facing roles.
  • In-depth knowledge of web service technologies, unit testing, CI/CD processes, and web performance optimization techniques.
  • Advanced proficiency in building systems such as Webpack, Grunt, Gulp, etc., and a strong understanding of security practices.
  • Take ownership of front-end architecture, actively participate in UX (user experience) design, and conduct thorough code reviews.
  • Prioritize software qualities like flexibility, scalability, security, and reusability.
  • Produce detailed design documentation, ensure adherence to development standards, and provide support to engineering teams.
  • Engage in prototyping and continuously enhance development processes.
  • Establish and maintain performance criteria for projects and promote collaboration among teams to improve code sharing, optimize website performance, and accelerate time-to-market.

What achievements do you attain?

  • Your contributions will play a pivotal role in achieving our business objectives, offering you valuable insights into your role and fostering personal growth opportunities.
  • You'll enjoy working in an excellent workspace located in the bustling IT hub of Bangalore, where you'll collaborate with outstanding colleagues.
  • We prioritize ongoing learning and development to keep you well-informed and up-to-date in your field.
  • You'll have the opportunity to work with talented and diverse teams, cultivating an environment that encourages creativity and innovation.

Inside the Position: Key Job Information

  • Number of available positions: 2
  • Work arrangement: On-site work
  • Preference for candidates who can join within 2-3 weeks
